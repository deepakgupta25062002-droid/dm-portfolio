FAQs

SEO Questions Answered

How long does SEO take to work? +
SEO is a long-term investment. Most clients start seeing measurable organic growth within 3–4 months. Significant results typically emerge at 6–8 months. The payoff is compounding: unlike paid ads, rankings you earn don't disappear the moment you stop spending.
Do you write the content or do we? +
We handle everything — keyword research, briefs, writing, optimisation, and publishing. We work with subject matter experts at your company to ensure accuracy, especially for technical or regulated industries. You review and approve before anything goes live.
What is E-E-A-T and why does it matter? +
Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness — Google's quality framework for evaluating content credibility. It matters enormously, especially in finance, health, and legal verticals. We build E-E-A-T signals into your content strategy, author profiles, and site architecture.
Can SEO work alongside paid ads? +
Absolutely — they complement each other powerfully. PPC gives you immediate visibility and conversion data, while SEO builds a free, compounding traffic channel. PPC keyword data can also directly inform your SEO content strategy. Most of our best-performing clients run both simultaneously.

FAQs

Social Media Questions Answered

Which social platforms should my brand be on? +
It depends entirely on where your audience is, not where everyone else is. B2B companies often see the best ROI on LinkedIn. DTC consumer brands typically perform best on Instagram and TikTok. We'll recommend the right mix after auditing your audience data — being on every platform often means being mediocre on all of them.
How often will you post? +
Cadence depends on the platform and your goals. Typically: Instagram 4–5x/week, TikTok 5–7x/week, LinkedIn 3–4x/week. Quality always trumps quantity — we'd rather post 3 high-performing pieces than 7 mediocre ones.
Do you handle video content? +
Yes. We can produce short-form video content including reels, TikToks, and YouTube Shorts — either from raw footage you provide, UGC creator content, or using motion graphics and AI-assisted tools. Full video production (camera crew, location shoots) can be arranged as an add-on.
How do you measure social media ROI? +
We track both vanity metrics (reach, engagement, followers) and business metrics (link clicks, lead form submissions, website sessions, and attributed conversions). For paid social, we use platform attribution plus third-party tools like Triple Whale for more accurate ROAS reporting.

FAQs

Email & CRM Questions Answered

Which email platform should I use? +
It depends on your business model. For e-commerce, Klaviyo is our top recommendation — deep Shopify integration and powerful segmentation. For B2B and SaaS, HubSpot or ActiveCampaign offer the best CRM-plus-email combination. We'll recommend the right platform after understanding your stack and goals.
We have a small list. Is email marketing worth it? +
Absolutely. A list of 1,000 highly engaged subscribers often outperforms a disengaged list of 50,000. We focus on list quality, segmentation, and delivering relevant content — which drives outsized results even at small scale. We also help you implement list growth strategies to build your audience over time.
How do you ensure emails don't go to spam? +
Deliverability is a discipline. We manage sender reputation, properly authenticate your domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), warm new sending domains gradually, maintain clean lists by removing disengaged contacts, and monitor inbox placement rates with tools like Litmus and MXToolbox.
How is email marketing different from CRM marketing? +
Email is a channel; CRM is the system that powers personalised communication across all channels. A CRM stores your customer data, tracks interactions, and enables segmentation — email is how you reach them. We connect both so your emails are personalised by real behaviour and purchase data, not just a name merge tag.
